Cloudiness



Some cities exist shrouded in fog and perennially covered in clouds. This is for our protection as mankind is not meant to know what lurks in these places. Those that have traveled too closely and could not keep from sating their curiousity seldom return. The unlucky few babble of abominations. The horror. The horror.
